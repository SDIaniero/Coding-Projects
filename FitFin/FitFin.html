What is FitFin?

Financial Planning is boring for employees. It's confusing. Often times, they give up on following through or taking advantage of all the benefits your company truly can offer them. Ultimately hurting your bottom-line and wasting everybodys time...and time is money.

FitFin means Fitness Financial is a SaaS company in which we incentivize your employees to take advantage of the financial planning options. It keeps them well-informed, up-to-date on their financials, reach financial goals and so much more.

Studies show that a major contributor of stress for individuals is often related to their financials. Stress-out employees = lower productivity and overall happiness. Why can't your company be the company that contributes to solving their financial woes in a fun, engaging and unique way?
